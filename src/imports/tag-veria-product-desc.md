Tag Veria
Product Description

Tag Veria is a broad-spectrum biological insecticide containing the highly virulent strain of Beauveria Bassiana, an entomopathogenic fungus that effectively controls a wide range of insect pests.

When the fungal spores (conidia) come in contact with the insect body, they attach to the insect cuticle and begin to germinate. This process causes a disease known as White Muscardine, which eventually kills the insect by draining its nutrients.

Tag Veria is eco-friendly, leaves no chemical residue, and is compatible with most agricultural inputs except chemical fungicides. It also contains a high spore count of minimum 100 million CFU per gram, ensuring strong efficacy.

Time of Application

Apply regularly at 20–30 day intervals, starting 20–25 days after sowing or planting, to maintain a pest-free crop.

Dosage per Acre

1 kg per acre with a suitable wetting agent like Kloud for better efficacy

Foliar Spray: 5 g per litre of water

Soil Drenching: 5 g per litre of water

Tag Veria can also be mixed with FYM (Farm Yard Manure) and broadcast in the field.

Visible pest mortality generally begins from the 3rd day after spraying.

Application Method

Use as a preventive (prophylactic) treatment

Apply when target pests begin to appear

For best results, spray in the late evening

Compatibility

Compatible with botanical and common agricultural chemicals, except chemical fungicides.

Precautions

Do not mix with chemical fungicides

If fungicides are applied, use Tag Veria after 7 days
