Gold Bionik
Product Description

Gold Bionik is a biofertilizer containing a beneficial mixture of mycorrhizae (VAM) spores – Glomus spp. with 2000 infective propagules per gram. These mycorrhizal inoculants quickly colonize plant roots after application and help improve plant growth and soil health.

Gold Bionik enhances soil structure, water retention, and stress tolerance while stimulating plant growth through improved nutrient uptake. It also enhances phytohormone activity and photosynthesis, leading to better crop growth and yield.

The product supports the synthesis of plant growth substances such as gibberellins, oximes, and cytokinins, making it particularly useful for the survival, growth, and establishment of micro-propagated plants.

Suitable Crops

Suitable for all crops.

Time of Application

Seed treatment

Seedling root dip

Drip irrigation application

Dosage per Acre

Seed Treatment: 2 – 10 g per kg seed (depending on seed size)

Seedling Root Dip: 1 – 2 g per litre of water

Drip Irrigation System: 100 g per acre

Application Method

Gold Bionik is 100% water soluble.

Mix the required quantity in water before application

Suitable for seed treatment, drip irrigation, and seedling root dip

Precautions

Do not mix with chemical fungicides, urea, or foliar fertilizers

Apply 7 days after the application of chemical fungicides
